# Olden Forge — Product Summary

## Vision

A companion toolbox for Heroes of Might and Magic: Olden Era players to theorycraft builds and learn game mechanics.

## Target Audience

HoMM: Olden Era players who want to plan strategies, optimize builds, and master game knowledge outside of gameplay.

## Core Features

- **Hero Builder** — Create and explore hero builds with skills, stats, and equipment
- **Unit Quiz** — Anki-style flashcards to learn units by icon (faction, tier, attack type)
- **Game Data Browser** — Browse heroes, units, skills, spells, and items with images
- **Game Data Import** — Sync data directly from local game installation
